The Best Movies According to Edgar

My top 3 movies

Ratatouille

A great animated movie that teaches you that your background should not inherently define what you can do.

Yes Man

Really funny movie about a guy (played by Jim Carrey) that chooses to say yes to every single oppotunity that comes his way.

Star Wars Episode IV: The Empire Strikes Back

I always have a blast watching this movie, and the twist is a classic.
